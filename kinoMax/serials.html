Walking Dead

The series tells the story of the life of the sheriff's family after the "zombies" - an epidemic of apocalyptic proportions swept the globe. Sheriff Rick Grime travels with his family and a small group of survivors in search of a safe place to live. But the constant fear of death every day brings heavy losses, forcing the heroes to feel the depths of human cruelty. Rick is trying to save his family, and discovers that the all-consuming fear of those who survived can be more dangerous than the senseless dead that roam the earth.

The X Files

In the series, FBI Special Agents Fox Mulder and Dana Scully work in the X-Files department, which specializes in strange unsolved cases related to the paranormal. Mulder believes in the existence of aliens and other supernatural things, while Scully, being a skeptic by nature, promotes a scientific approach in everything and often disagrees with the theories of her partner [4]. A close bond develops between the characters, beginning as a platonic friendship and ending in a romantic relationship.
